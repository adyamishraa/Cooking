Main Course

    Roasted Bang Bang Shrimp

Ingredients

  • For the Shrimp:
    1. 1 lb of Shrimp, peeled and deveined
    2. 2 tsp of Granulated Garlic
    3. 1 tsp of Paprika
    4. 1 Tbsp of Olive Oil
    5. Salt and Pepper, to taste
  • For the sauce:
    1. 1/2 cup of Mayo
    2. 2 Tbsp of Plain Greek Yogurt
    3. 1-1/2 Tbsp of Srirarcha (or to taste)
    4. 1/4 cup of Sweet Chili Sauce
    5. Pinch of Salt
    6. Chopped Scallions

    • Preparation

    1. To make the sauce, stir together the mayo, yogurt, Srirarcha, chili sauce, salt and scallions, cover with some plastic wrap and pop it in the fridge for a bit.
    2. Preheat your oven to 450 degrees. Toss the shrimp with the oil, granulated garlic, paprika, salt and pepper, lay on a single layer and pop them in the oven for about 8 to 10 minutes.
    3. When ready to serve, sprinkle some additional chopped scallions over the shrimp and serve along side the sauce!
